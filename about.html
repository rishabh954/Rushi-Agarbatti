About Us

INCENSE STICK SUPPLIER, MANUFACTURER, DEALER AND DISTRIBUTOR IN INDIA

Having been in the industry of incense sticks manufacturing since 2000, Rushi Agarbatti is a name that has earned the respect, goodwill and faith of its consumers and competitors alike. The Founders, Vrajesh Darji through their hard work, dedication, farsightedness, and dynamism made the company into one of the most trusted and popular brands of our times.

At Rushi Agarbatti, we believe that fragrance has the power to soothe the mind and uplift the soul. With years of expertise in crafting high-quality agarbattis, we blend tradition with innovation to create long-lasting, eco-friendly incense sticks. Our mission is to bring you purity, relaxation, and a sense of divine connection through our aromatic creations.
